Savannah: Old Town Hop-on Hop-off Trolley Tour

The Old Town Trolley Tour in Savannah, Georgia, is a popular way to explore the city's rich history and scenic beauty. Known for its charming streets, historic landmarks, and Southern hospitality, Savannah offers visitors a unique perspective on its past through this guided tour. The trolley allows passengers to hop on and off at various locations, providing the flexibility to explore key attractions at their own pace. Stops include highlights like Forsyth Park, with its iconic fountain, and the historic squares that dot the city, each with its own story and charm. Guests can visit sites such as the Cathedral of St. John the Baptist, River Street, and the Pirates' House, one of Savannahâ€™s oldest buildings. Along the way, guides share stories about the city's colonial roots, Civil War history, and famous residents like Juliette Gordon Low, founder of the Girl Scouts of the USA. The tour is a great way to experience Savannah's unique blend of history, architecture, and natural beauty.
