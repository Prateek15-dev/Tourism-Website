Cruises and Boats tours

Savannah:Riverboat Sight Cruise

Sail along Savannah's beautiful waterfront on a Savannah Riverboat. Discover Savannah's history via live commentary, and even experience the thrill of hearing the Old Fort Jackson cannons.

Hilton Head Island

Take Hilton Head Island’s only express ferry service to Savannah, Georgia to discover the city. Dock directly on River Street and start exploring right away. Use your round-trip ferry ticket to return to Hilton Head Island at the end of your day.

Tybee Island:Tiki Boat Sight Cruise

Embark on a Tiki boat tour off Tybee Island's coast, spotting playful dolphins and enjoying scenic views. Experience the local marine life and history with an expert captain.

Nature and adventure

Savannah: Sunset Dolphin

Discover the Savannah River on a sunset dolphin-watching eco trip by boat. Enjoy the narration from the captain, admire the marine wildlife, and watch the sun go down as you sail back to port.

Atlanta: Georgia Aquarium

Enjoy skip-the-line tickets to the Georgia Aquarium in Atlanta with 7 different galleries containing more than 10 million gallons of water and thousands of sea creatures.

Savannah:Dolphin Tour

Head to Tybee Island by trolley/bus for a delicious lunch, dolphin cruise, and ocean view photo opportunities.

Family Friendly activities

Atlanta: World of Coca-Cola

Visit World of Coca-Cola and immerse yourself in the rich history of the iconic drink surrounded by more than 1,200 pieces of memorabilia and archival elements.

Holiday Sights and Festive Nights

Get into the Christmas spirit on this holiday lights and sights trolley tour around Savannah’s most festive spots. Let conductor elves delight and entertain you with tales of holiday traditions.

Atlanta: Fun Spot

Choose from over 20+ rides in the park from daring heights to classic rides like the Ferris Wheel. Enjoy unlimited rides at Georiga's only go-kart track.

Day Trips

Atlanta: 'The Walking Dead'

Explore filming locations of TWD by selecting from sites in ATL or venturing beyond the city to the towns of Woodbury and Alexandria. For a comprehensive tour that includes both, see TWD VIP tour

Boo Y'all Comedy Ghost Party Bus

Embark on a hilarious journey through Savannah’s haunted past on the boo y'all comedy ghost tour party bus. Explore Savannah’s dark side at locations like the Green-Meldrim House and Wright Square.

Half-Day or Full-Day Wine Country

Head out of Atlanta and embark on a half-day or full-day trip by bus to the North Georgia Wine Country. Visit two to three celebrated wineries and enjoy 9-12 wine tastings.

Culture and History

Wormsloe and Bonaventure

Learn about Southern history by touring a plantation. Admire ornate mansions set against the Georgia marsh. Discover Bonaventure Cemetery to learn more about the lives of notable inhabitants.

Civil War History Pub Crawl

Delve into the heart of Savannah to hear the lesser-known stories of the Civil War on this walking tour. Stop for refreshing handcrafted cocktails at a variety of upscale establishments on the way.

History and Sightseeing Trolley

Go through 270 years of American history on a 90-minute trolley tour of one of the largest National Urban Historic Landmarked Districts in America.

Shows and musicals

Savannah for Morons

Have fun on this comedy show on wheels, hosted by the infamous Moron twins. Enjoy a quirky, saucy and eccentric Savannah history lesson featuring comedy skits, musical numbers, and costume changes.

Buffet Dinner-Live Entertainment

Embark on a dinner cruise with live entertainment down the scenic Savannah River. Enjoy the food, the music, and the incredible views for an unforgettable evening.

Worlds of Puppetry Museum

Enjoy a self-guided adventure through the Center for Puppetry Arts, Worlds of Puppetry Museum. Explore the Interactive Jim Henson collection with your favorite characters.

Hidden Gems

Drag Queen Guided Pub

Experience the fabulous side of life on this outrageously fun drag queen-guided pub crawl. Visit some of Savannah’s best bars and clubs for dancing and sing-a-longs with a world-class drag queen.

Cocktail Class

Join an expert bartender for a cocktail learning experience in an authentic speakeasy. Learn cocktail history while sipping custom-made drinks and sampling the classics.

True Crime Pub Crawl

Take a thrilling journey through the grisliest crimes ever committed. Hear it all on a guided walking tour to some of the most famous pubs in the city, from stories of destined-to-death lovers to homicidal partners.
